Privacy Policy for PDF Editor by ib

Last Updated: July 2026

Important: This is a template structure. Legal content must be reviewed and customized by legal counsel before production launch.

1. Information Collection

PDF Editor by ib is a browser-based application. We collect minimal data:

  • Local Storage: Preferences and settings stored on your device
  • Session Data: Temporary data for current session only
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect names, emails, or personally identifiable information

2. Local Processing

All PDF processing occurs entirely in your browser. PDFs are:

  • Never uploaded to our servers
  • Never stored on external servers
  • Processed locally on your device only

3. Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • User preferences and settings
  • Session management (if applicable)

You may disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

4. Analytics

If analytics are enabled (e.g., Google Analytics), they track:

  • Page views
  • User interactions
  • Device and browser information

Note: Analytics implementation and service details will be specified before launch.

5. Third-Party Services

PDF Editor by ib may use third-party services such as:

  • Content Delivery Networks (CDN)
  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks (AdSense)

Each third-party service maintains its own privacy policy. Please review their policies separately.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • HTTPS encryption for all communications
  • Local-first processing model
  • No server-side data storage
